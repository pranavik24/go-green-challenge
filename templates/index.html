Problems from Plastic

In our current world, pollution has become rampant, with plastic pollution as one of the leading causes. Plastic is especially dangerous, because it is photodegradable, rather than biodegradable. This means that it can take thousands of years for just one plastic bottle to degrade. In addition, due to improper disposal of trash, such as littering, a lot of plastic enters our forests and oceans, destroying environments and killing animals.

Why Recycling Matters

Rather than allowing one plastic bottle to have a single lifetime as a bottle, recycling allows the bottle to be repurposed, and then reused for future water bottles. This leads to less production of plastic, and reduces the amount of plastic waste.

Our Initiative

Only 8% of plastics are recycled in the United States, of the 37.5 millions tons of plastics produced each year. Here at Go Green!, we want to motivate people to increase the amount of plastic water bottles they recycle through a friendly competition, vying for the top spot of the greatest water bottles recycled. And in the process, we can all help the planet, one bottle at a time! Why don't you join the fun!!
